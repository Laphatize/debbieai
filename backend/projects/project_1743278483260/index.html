Welcome to the College of Engineering

The Penn State College of Engineering is the engineering school of the Pennsylvania State University. It was established in 1896, under the leadership of George W. Atherton. Today, it is one of the largest engineering schools in the United States, with over 13,000 students enrolled in its undergraduate and graduate programs.

Our Programs

The Penn State College of Engineering offers more than 25 undergraduate degree programs and more than 40 graduate degree programs in various fields of engineering.
